Traditionally, high jewelry has been associated with author jewelry (auteur jewelry), in which behind every gem there is a well-known artisan whose sole gnature is enough to relate the jewel to the ideas of quality and luxury.

For Mountaber, high jewelry is something else. It is the sum of talent, research and effort of artisans throughout centuries of work. All of this confers a unique nature and jewelry culture to each corner of the world.
