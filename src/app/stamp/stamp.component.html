Mountaber

We are named after the hill on which the Romans settled in Barcelona, Mont Taber (Mount Taber). Because the fact is that the origins of Civilization are also the origins of our jewelry tradition, an art which grew thanks to the rise of guilds between the 12th and 14th centuries. As well as to the legacy that certain artistic movements, such as Catalan Modernism, have left.
